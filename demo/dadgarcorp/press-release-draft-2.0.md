## DadgarCorp Launches Timberline 2.0, Bringing Golden-Path Environments to Every Developer

*New Environment Blueprints cut infrastructure wait times from days to minutes; enterprise identity and audit arrive for regulated teams*

SAN FRANCISCO — **October 1, 2026** — DadgarCorp, the internal developer platform company, today announced the general availability of Timberline 2.0. The release introduces Environment Blueprints — Terraform-native golden paths that let platform teams define a compliant environment once and let any developer create their own copy in under ten minutes.

"Platform engineering fails when the paved road is slower than the dirt path," said **Priya Raman, Chief Product Officer at DadgarCorp**. "Timberline 2.0 makes the compliant way the fast way. That's the whole trick."

At Meridian Bank, a Timberline design partner, environment requests that previously took more than three days now complete in minutes. "Our developers stopped filing infrastructure tickets," said the bank's Director of Platform Engineering. "They just... make environments. Correctly. It's eerie."

### What's new in 2.0

- **Environment Blueprints** — author golden-path environments with built-in policy guardrails, cost caps, and automatic teardown
- **Enterprise identity** — SAML SSO and SCIM provisioning with Okta and Microsoft Entra ID
- **Audit and compliance** — immutable audit logs with export, plus one-click SOC 2 evidence bundles

### Pricing and availability

Timberline 2.0 is generally available today. The new **Pro tier is $69 per developer per month** and includes policy guardrails and teardown automation; enterprise capabilities including SSO and audit export are available in the Enterprise plan. Existing customers keep their current pricing through renewal.

DadgarCorp serves more than 61,000 weekly active developers across 480 customers, including four of the ten largest logistics companies in North America.
